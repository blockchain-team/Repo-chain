Vehicle Industry Token——VIT

  • Vehicle Industry Token, abbreviated as "VIT", is the token of vehicle industry.
  • VIT is digital asset token based on ACChain tool of asset digitization, it is mainly used in transaction aggregation and settlement after asset digitization in vehicle industry.
  • VIT can be used to redeem vehicles and related products, achieve the circulation of assets with equal value. During the appreciation of VIT, holders can also exchange it with real assets in corresponding programs, completing asset allocation.

Program Introduction

Ecological construction of vehicle industry token VIT

Energy vehicles and zgxcw.com belongs to different program under the vehicle industry token VIT, investors can redeem energy vehicles and the auto services of zgxcw.com by VIT; more autos and side products/services will be launched online to enrich ecology of vehicle industry token VIT and the circulation of industrial resources.

Issuing Principle and Exit Mechanism of Vehicle Industry Token VIT

1. Total value of issuance:
Programs worthy 1000 million RMB is announced in this round of publicity, the procedures such as identification and evaluation, insurance and trusteeship have been finished.In the corresponding programs, 1000 million coins of vehicle industry tokens VIT will be issued.
2. Exit Mechanism:
1）. VIT token holders can redeem vehicles or auto services of zgxcw. com by VIT tokens according to their requirements and the corresponding prices.
2）. VIT holders can exchange VIT in trading platform (exchange with other tokens or sell VIT in digital asset trading platform to withdraw).
3）. VIT tokens can be exchanged into NPC - digital standard token in China area, to fulfill the requirement of exchange and circulation for more assets by the exchange media of NPC.
