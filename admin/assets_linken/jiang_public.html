Originated from local superior glutinous sorghum,"Xiao'hong'liang kun'zi puree draft liquor" distills yeast of high temperature. The content of distiller's yeast is higher than that of sorghum. It is made uniquely by a high content of distiller's yeast, a long period of fermentation, a high frequency of fermentation and distilling. That is why Hua'yang Jiang-flavor liquor is special in style and superior in quality. The process of brewing includes two times of adding sorghum, nine times of steaming, eight times of airing and adding yeast(seven times of fermentation) and seven times of distilling. The production cycle takes a year and storage takes more than three years. After blending and leveling, it will take one more year to stockpile. The texture of liquor would be more harmonious and much mellower , it will be softer and tenderer before being packed in the bottle and leaving factory. The whole production process takes nearly five years.
Ever since the founding of People's Republic of China, the Jiang-flavor liquor has been sent to foreign leaders as a national gift in countless major events. Through the ages, many poets and artists showed their interest and admiration to the Jiang-flavor liquor. It is no exaggeration to say that every detailed "drop" of the Jiang-flavor liquor is contained with abundant stories of art history, it is filled with cultural accumulation and humanistic value.
