RET ICO Operation Guide

1.Login to the ACC official website: www.acchain.org，Click “Join Now”

2.There are three options for you on the drop-down box.

  (The minimum investment is 1 RET-SCHSR, 1 RET-SCHSR=1 USD)

2.1 Join by digital asset

(1)ACC holders login to the official web (www.acchain.org). Click into “Join Now”, there will be a participant address of ICO.

(2)Firstly, enter the official web. Then click “My” →“Transfer-out asset”. Thirdly, enter the transfer-out address (your wallet address) and the amount （1 ACC = 0.5 RET, the minimum amount is 1 RET）. Finally, submit it. If your ACC was in the wallet, please transfer them directly.

(3)If you want to use BTC or ETH for RET ICO, you should transfer your coins to the RET ICO address from the exchange platform or your wallet.

2.2 Join by Cash

Use “Paypal” to participate.
(1) “http://www.pencilblockchain.com/”will appear when you click “Join Now”. Enter the “Project” on the navigation bar or drop down in this page.

If you had problems in Paypal registration, please open the http://www.paypal.com/cn/home in browser.

(2)Select the “Project” of RET-SCHSR. Then click “Join Us”.

(3)Click “PURCHASE” .

(4)Please read “Paypal Guide” carefully, and then click “buy now” to enter the payment page.

Click BUY NOW to enter payment page.
https://www.paypal.me/pencilblockchain

(5)Enter the money (USD) that you want to invest, and click “Next”.

(6) The registered user can login to your Paypal account directly.

PS: Unregistered users can register firstly, (the registered user can skip this step).
A. Login to www.paypal.com, click “Sign up” and “Personal Account”.

If you had problems in the registration, please open the
http://www.paypal.com/cn/home in browser.

B. Please complete the registration information, and click “Next”

C. Please complete the information and click “Agree and Create Account”.

(7)Confirm the order and add a note for your address of ACChain digital wallet. You don`t need to fill in your delivery address.
