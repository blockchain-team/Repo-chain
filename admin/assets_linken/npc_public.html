What is NPC?

NPC stands for New Puer Coin. NPC is the standard digital token and issued base on Tibetan tea. The high quality Tibetan tea has stored by third party, evaluated, authenticated, and right authenticated. Also, the Tibetan tea has digitized through ACChain. Digital asset token is issued through blockchain under line technology and digital encryption.
