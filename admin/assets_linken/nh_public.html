Ning'hong gan'hong tea token issuer

Ning'hong Group of Jiang'xi Province
Ning'hong group is an old-branded and the major leading corporation in agriculture industrialization from China, its precursor Xiu'shui tea factory was built in 1949, and became a comprehensive group company with production, supply, sale and research after decades of development. Ning'hong cultural industrial park is the headquarters of the re-organized Ning'hong Group, covers an area of 160 acres. In the industrial park, there is a production workshop with construction area of 10,000 square meters, in which Ning'hong black tea, popular green tea, Ning'hong health tea and some major tea are produced in it. It has provided an insurance and foundation to the promotion of the value and brand of Ning'hong. Ning'hong group has a very powerful research and develop team and a strong ability to control the quality.It controls quality of the whole process from seeding, growing in the tea park, and tea in the cup. It also has the QS enterprise food production license, ISO22000 certification.
