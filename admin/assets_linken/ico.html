ACC ICO Policy
  • ICO OBJECT: BTC、ETH
  • ICO PERIOD：MAY 9th, 2017——June 6th, 2017, 28 days period.
  • ICO RULES：

    The goal of ICO is to raise 100 million units of ACC, 1,000 units of ACC is the minimum investing number in the ICO. (Note: If the number of ACC invested is lower than 1000, this operation will be considered as invalid and the BTC/ETH invested will not be refunded).

     
  • Fund Distribution:

    The number of investment in BTC for this ICO is set to be 11111 unit, among which, 20% of digital asset will be used as incentives for delegates and 80% of digital asset will be locked in asset pool through smart contract of blockchain. With this ICO we hope to structure DAO (decentralized autonomous organizations) and to manage the raised capital from ICO. The capital will be managed by all "peerages" members, each transaction will have to be approved by 51% of the members. With the value of ACChain (asset collection chain) increasing, the value of ACC will rise significantly as well.

  • ACChain's DAO：

    The DAO of ACChain community is based on the global distributional autonomous organizations in Blockchain ecosystem, it is constituted and managed by the "Peerage" members, Peerages will participate in community’s decision making and voting. With more and more ACChain global supernodes being built, ACChain community’s DAO will become a global distributional autonomous organization. DAO is managed by all “ peerage” members, and in the form of DAO to implement an open management. Through the incentive mechanism, it can attract more “ peerage” to join DAO. “ Peerage” members can be rewarded by fulfill his duties as a member.

  • Peerage Policy：
    1.Possess 10,000+ ACC can apply for Peerage membership, and the application will be handed to community members for voting, all assets holders can participate in voting, once becomes a Peerage, applicant will be also recruited into DAO.
    2.Only with above 50% of the Peerages’ approval, applicant can become the new Peerage （The first batch of Peerages will be nominated during this ICO）.
    3.Reward &Punishment: According to the self-management treaty, any contribution will be rewarded with ACC, and any violation will cause ACC deduction.
    4.Exit: Failed to apply self - management treaty can lead to expulsion from the community with 2/3rd of the Peerages’ voting.
  • Peerage Ranking：

    The first batch of Peerages will be nominated during this ICO, and the Peerage ranking system will be established by the first batch of Peerages.
