Designing Mechanism
In order to break down the technology barrier of Blockchain in commercialization, RET is providing following solutions :
1. Establish the asset digitization standards in real estate industry, and introducing the Smart Contract protocol to execute the contract by combining the on chain and off chain data as the triggering condition.
2. Providing a new business model with a compliant the asset digitization process.
RET's Vision
RET is dedicated to the purpose of establishing the standards for real estate assets digitization, and to build a worldwide open-source real estate industrial community eco-system, applying the cutting-edgy blockchain technology to the traditional industry.

RET Issuing Plan and Exit Mechanism

RET Issuing Plan
RET will be locked in the RET pool upon the completion of generation, and will be released by exchanging with other digital assets.
RET's consumption can stimulate the issuing of Project tokens under the RET system.
There are 100 billion units of RET in asset pool which will be released base on the asset exchange. The first exchange amount is 50 million units. With the increasing number of real estate issuing and market requirements in American and the UK, The RET in asset pool will be exchanged in from the pool. The degree of difficulty will increase for each period exchange.
RET Exit Mechanism
There are three ways to exit for RET holders：
1.RET can be traded on the trading platforms;
2.RET can be exchanged into other digital assets;
3.RET can be redeemed to real estate properties , product or services of equal value;
Redemption Policy：
Different regions and industries will have difference Redemption Policy, RET can be redeemed into properties and products of equal value according to individual market's standard.
Allowable Coin： BTC 、ETH 、 ACC
