Project Introduction

Real Estate Token or RET is an encrypted digital token that represents digitized real estate assets, generated based on ACChain open source blockchain technology. Through RET, property owners and real estate developers can widen the reach of their distribution and provide a hassle-free experience to investors across the globe.

RET can significantly reduce the development costs and construction time for developers, helping the company to grow and expand faster.

For buyers, RET provides participant greater transparency as relevant project information such as cost performance, content authenticity and asset status are readily available in the platform. This also helps buyers to make an informed decision at the shortest time possible. Moreover, RET offers better liquidity for real estate assets.

What’s RET SCH-SR?
RET, or Real Estate Token, is an encrypted digital token that represents a digitized real estate asset, generated on ACChain using the open-source blockchain technology. RETs are issued by MGI (Meiyingju Group International Company Limited), a Hong Kong-based company.
Real Estate Developer
Serene Country Homes is an up-and-coming American property developer based in Dallas- Fort Worth, Texas. One of their major residential project, Serene Sendera Ranch, serves as the underlying asset behind RET. The 889.53-acre project will be developed in 10 phases, and will deliver 2385 homes with a total value of approximately USD $655mm.

RET Distribution Mechanism & Exit Mechanism

Distribution Mechanism:
1.Total Volume :
With 2385 houses valued at an average of USD$275,000 each, the total value of the development is approximately USD$655mm.
2.ICO project:
Based on the approximate value of the development, 655,875,000tokens will be issued in 2017, with each token valued as USD $ 1. Two days after the RET sales period, the next phase will starts.
3.RET distribution channel：Primary Market、Digital Asset Fund and ICO
Exit Mechanism
There will be a fixed number of RET issued for each property. For Sendera Ranch, a total of 655,875 ,000 SR-RET were issued based on the current home valuation of 2385 homes in Sendera Ranch.
RET Holders will be able to use the tokens in the following ways:
1- Hold it as an investment – Real Estate is still one of the best investment classes in the world. As the value of the token is linked to the value of the property, token holders directly benefit from the appreciation of the property without the hassle of maintaining one.
2- Purchase of a home – Token holders opt to purchase a home in Sendera Ranch using their SR-RETs once they have accumulated enough tokens equivalent to the value of a home.
3- Trading – Token holders can also exchange their RETs for other tokens or sell it on trading platforms
4- Purchase of other RETs – Token holders can also use their RETs to pay for RETs of other properties within the ACChain’s real estate asset community.
5- Other Purchase Transactions - An ecosystem for RET is currently being planned. Once established, there will be infinite application scenarios that would allow RET holders to use their tokens to pay for goods and services from various merchants and retailers.

ICO Policy

The assets behind RET is using USD as payment unit, therefore, ICO’s goal will also use USD as reference value. All digital assets that participated in the ICO will be converted to equivalent RET using JUBI platform’s real time exchange rate between the digital asset used and the USD, based on the participating time, type of the digital asset, and amount. When RET is released, which occurs upon the closing of the ICO, and corresponding quantity of RET will be transferred to the participator’s ACChain digital wallet. The minimum “buy-in” is 1 RET during the ICO period, lower than 1 RET will be considered as invalid operation.
